Tourist Spots in Baguio City

Burnham Park

A serene park in the heart of Baguio, perfect for boat rides, biking,

and leisurely walks. This is a park where also almost anyone can

access since it free and open to the public. You can go here at December

to experience a snow-like feeling and vibe due to the Christmas event held here.

Camp John Hay

A historic retreat known for its scenic trails, golf course, and luxury accommodations.

The Mansion

The official summer residence of the Philippine President, surrounded by beautiful gardens.

Botanical Garden

A lush green sanctuary showcasing local flora, art, and indigenous Cordilleran heritage.

Wright Park

Famous for horseback riding and its beautiful pine tree-lined pathways.

Tam-awan Village

A reconstructed traditional Cordilleran village that serves as an art and cultural center.

La Trinidad Strawberry Farm

A must-visit spot where visitors can experience strawberry picking in the cool highlands.
